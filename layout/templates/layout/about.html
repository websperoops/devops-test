OUR CUSTOMER

It takes an exhausting few years to build a successful e-commerce business from the ground up. Transforming a talent into a great brand is no easy feat, nonetheless scaling to a point of consistently meeting demand as you find success. We recognized the lack of quality, affordable data analytics tools specifically for e-commerce, making this transformation from talent to brand even more difficult. That's why we built the Blocklight Analytics Platform - to help online businesses sell more, easier.

Blocklight officially formed on March 8, 2018. Today, a talented core team, along with many skilled designers, developers, advisors, and partners, are working to bring Blocklight's unique offering to the world.
