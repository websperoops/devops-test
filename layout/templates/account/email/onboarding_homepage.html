Getting to know Blocklight

Homepage

The Homepage is one of the most useful pages in Blocklight! It has your Business Timeline, Summary Metrics, and Top Posts & Stories! This is a great page to keep open or check frequently while you’re busy running your business!

The Business Timeline has everything from your recent orders and returns, abandoned checkouts, Mailchimp campaign information, Google Analytics website visits, and much more!

Summary Metrics give heads up information about what’s going on that’s important to your business; sales, refunds, website visitors, campaign information, and more!

Top Posts & Stories give you information about your page’s most successful posts and stories in terms of engagement and reach within your community.

Ensure you never lose touch with the pulse of your business or let anything fall through the cracks with this tool and the many others at your disposal in Blocklight!
